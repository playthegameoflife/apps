Common Mistakes

Even with powerful tools at your disposal, it is easy to fall into traps that degrade the quality of your output. Being aware of these common pitfalls is crucial for maintaining a professional standard in your work.

  • Mistake 1: Overloading the prompt with conflicting styles. Many users try to combine "photorealistic" with "cartoon" and "oil painting" simultaneously, which confuses the model and results in muddy, incoherent images. A professional generator helps prevent this by suggesting compatible style tags.
  • Mistake 2: Ignoring negative prompts. A major reason for failed generations is the lack of a robust negative prompt to filter out unwanted artifacts like "bad hands," "blurry," or "distorted faces." Professional tools automatically append comprehensive negative lists to ensure clean results.
