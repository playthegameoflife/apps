Common Mistakes

Even with the best tools, artists often stumble when transitioning from traditional methods to AI-assisted workflows. Avoiding these pitfalls will save you time and frustration.

  • Mistake 1: Relying solely on vague prompts. Even uncensored AI needs direction; failing to specify the artistic style or camera angle can lead to generic results that don't fit your comic's aesthetic.
