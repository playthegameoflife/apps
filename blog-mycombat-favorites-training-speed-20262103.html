Common Mistakes

  • Mistake 1: Hoarding too many favorites. When you save everything, you end up with a cluttered list that defeats the purpose of speed, forcing you to scroll and search just as much as before.
  • Mistake 2: Failing to update your list as your camp progresses. Using a heavy bag power-timer when you are currently focusing on speed and agility drills creates a mismatch in training stimulus and slows down your specific adaptation.
