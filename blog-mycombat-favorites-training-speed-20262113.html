Common Mistakes

Even with powerful tools like MyCombat, athletes often fall into traps that negate the efficiency gains. Here is what you need to avoid:

  • Mistake 1: Hoarding too many Favorites. It is tempting to save every single variation you try, but having 50 different favorites creates "choice paralysis." When you open the app and see a wall of options, you waste mental energy deciding which one to pick. Stick to your top 5-7 core routines that cover 90% of your training needs.
  • Mistake 2: Ignoring the warm-up integration. Many users save their main workout as a favorite but forget to include a dedicated warm-up round in that sequence. This leads to jumping straight into high-intensity intervals cold, which increases injury risk. Always ensure your Favorite includes a 3-5 minute dynamic warm-up timer at the start.
