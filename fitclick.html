Tips and Best Practices for FitClick

Consistency is Key

Use FitClick regularly, ideally 3-5 times per week. Consistent workouts lead to better results than sporadic intense sessions. The app's gamification features help maintain motivation.

Warm Up Before Workouts

Always start with a 5-10 minute warm-up before your FitClick workout. This prepares your body for exercise and reduces the risk of injury.

Listen to Your Body

Pay attention to how your body feels during workouts. If you experience pain (not just muscle fatigue), take a break. FitClick's personalized tasks adapt to your fitness level.

Mix Up Your Categories

Don't stick to just one category. Rotate between Cardio, Strength Training, and Core & Abs for a well-rounded fitness routine that targets all muscle groups.

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after your workouts. Proper hydration is essential for optimal performance and recovery.

Rest and Recovery

Allow your body time to recover between workouts. Rest days are just as important as workout days for muscle growth and overall fitness improvement.
